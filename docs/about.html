About Watson Memorials

Watson Memorials is a Darlington based Monumental Masons providing New Memorials, Cleaning, Restoration and Maintenance for all styles of Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones.

Craig Watson

Watson Memorials has been created through a passion for delivering the highest quality Memorial Masonry.

Craig Watson, the owner of Watson Memorials spent many years working with one of the area’s most well-known Monumental Masons. During his time with them, Craig undertook all aspects of modern Monumental Masonry, from the very first contact right through to the fitting or restoration of Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones.

LinkedIn

The Watson Memorials logo plays a huge part in representing the company. The design is based on research centred around traditional mason marks that were used to identify a masons work. The logo had to represent not only the company but Craig himself, a chance to leave his mark on carefully crafted memorials. Tradition had to meet modern design standards.

Why Watson Memorials

There are many Monumental Masons across County Durham and North Yorkshire, so why are Watson Memorials the one to choose?

With Craig’s passion for delivering only the very best service and products it is safe to say your family or friends Memorial will be treated with the utmost respect. Watson Memorials will take the time to fully understand your requirements and balance these with a vast knowledge of the regions Cemeteries and Churchyards.

Respect of Privacy

You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration; thus where possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
