Headstone and Memorial Photo Plaques

Ceramic photo plaques for headstones and memorials. We stock a wide range of sizes and shapes, many of which are available with standard frames or sandblast design frames. Some of our oval and round frames also have the option of a gold or silver filet border.

Respect of Privacy

You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration; thus where possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
