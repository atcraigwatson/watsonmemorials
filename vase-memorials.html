    Vase Memorials

    Watson Memorials provides several different shapes and sizes of Memorial Vases to suit being used on their own or in conjunction with a Headstone, Plaque or Kerb Set.

    PLEASE NOTE: All memorial vases can be crafted in any material, the decorative designs on the vases are shown to illustrate possibilities and do not have to be on your chosen memorial. For example, the Barrel Vase can be crafted in Black Granite without the decorative carving.

    Respect of Privacy

    You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration, thus were possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
