    Churchyard Memorials

    Watson Memorials provides a comprehensive selection of Memorials to suit Churchyard regulations. These stones are however still suitable for use in a Cemetery and can be created in any material.

    PLEASE NOTE: All memorials can be crafted in ANY material, the decorative designs on the memorials are shown to illustrate possibilities and DO NOT have to be on your chosen memorial. For example the Amplethorpe can be crafted in York Stone without the decorative carving.

    Respect of Privacy

    You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration, thus were possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
