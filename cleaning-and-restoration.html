    Cleaning & Restoration

    Watson Memorials provides a professional cleaning and restoration service for all styles of Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones.

    What Cleaning and Restoration Services Are Available for Headstones

    Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones of all styles are exposed to many elements that can cause a deterioration in, or appear on the surface of the material.

    Watson Memorials can provide a number of Cleaning or Restorative services including:

    • Marble Cleaning
    • York Stone and Sandstone Cleaning
    • Polished and Rustic Granite Cleaning
    • Letter Restoration, Re-painting or Re-Gilding

    If you feel the above do not match your requirements please call to discuss your specific memorial in more detail.

    All cleaning or restorative services include new flower containers.
