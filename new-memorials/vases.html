Creating Vase Memorials

Vase Memorials are available for use individually or in conjunction with a headstone, kerb memorial or plaque. There are many different sizes and styles of vase memorial however vases can be custom designed and crafted.

All memorials can be crafted in any material, the decorative designs on the memorials are shown to illustrate possibilities and do not have to be on your chosen memorial.
