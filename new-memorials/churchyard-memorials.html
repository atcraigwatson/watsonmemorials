Designing Churchyard Memorials

When designing headstones for churchyards, the first step is always to find out what regulations the churchyard has in place for the erection of a memorial. Many Churchyards have rules specific to that Churchyard alone, the memorials found on this page cover the wider spectrum to give a good variety of design ideas and to help illustrate possibilities.

All memorials can be crafted in any material, the decorative designs on the memorials are shown to illustrate possibilities and do not have to be on your chosen memorial.

Shortlisting Memorials

Due to the number of headstones and memorials displayed on the Watson Memorials website, you can now utilise a shortlisting feature by clicking the "+" icon in the top right of the memorial.

Whenever you need to, be sure to use margin utilities to keep things nice and tidy.
