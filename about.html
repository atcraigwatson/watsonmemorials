    About Watson Memorials

    Watson Memorials is a Darlington based Monumental Masons specialising in all aspects of Cleaning, Restoration and Maintenance associated with Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones.

    Craig Watson

    Watson Memorials has been created through a passion for delivering the highest quality Memorial Masonry. Craig Watson, the owner of Watson Memorials spent a number of years working with one of the area’s most well-known Monumental Masons. During his time with them, Craig undertook all aspects of modern Monumental Masonry, from the very first contact right through to the fitting or restoration of Headstones, Memorials and Gravestones.

    Respect of Privacy
