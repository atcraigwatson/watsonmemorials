    Lawn Memorials

    Watson Memorials provides an expansive selection of Lawn Memorials, commonly known as a standard headstones. Lawn memorials come in a vast array of shapes and sizes, as with all memorials, they can be bespoke to suit your preferences.

    PLEASE NOTE: All memorial headstones can be crafted in any material, the decorative designs on the memorials are shown to illustrate possibilities and do not have to be on your chosen memorial. For example, the Ainthorpe can be crafted in Black Granite without the decorative carving. You also have the option to raise flower containers using Block Vases.

    Respect of Privacy

    You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration, thus were possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
