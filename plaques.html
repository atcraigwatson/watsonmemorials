    Memorial Plaques and Desks

    Watson Memorials provides several different shapes, sizes and style of Memorial Plaque to suit being used on their own or in conjunction with a Headstone or Kerb Set.

    PLEASE NOTE: All memorial plaques and desks can be crafted in any material, the decorative designs on the plaues are shown to illustrate possibilities and do not have to be on your chosen memorial. For example, the Desk with Carved Book can be crafted in Blue Pearl Granite without the decorative carving. Desks with flats for flower containers also work well with Block Vases.

    Respect of Privacy

    You may notice that throughout this website, other than new memorials, there are no images of fitted memorials in Cemeteries or Churchyards. Watson Memorials has chosen to take the privacy of those passed into the utmost consideration, thus were possible a graphical representation of a product or services has been used rather than an image.
